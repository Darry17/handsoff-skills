# Section patterns

Content signals for landing page sections:
- Hero: Large title, single primary action, top of page.
- Navbar: Links, logo, horizontal top placement.
- Features: Grid/list of icons with text.
- Testimonials: Quotations and avatars.
- Pricing: Tables or tiered boxes with currency.
- FAQ: Accordions or list of questions.
- Footer: Sitemap, copyright, bottom of page.
